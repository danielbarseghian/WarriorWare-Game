# Linus Tech Games

### Description
Build your own PC by gathering parts and then assembling them to create a working computer!

<img width="535" height="299" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/04ef4d97-c168-40aa-9111-685ae56832b4" />


### Minigames
1. **Gather the Parts** — Walk through all four parts scattered around the map in less than 10 seconds to win.
2. **Save Your Parts** — All your parts have fallen! Grab them quickly before someone steals them. Click on all four parts before time runs out.
3. **Build Your PC** — Time to assemble everything and enjoy your finished build. Click a part, then click its matching area — you can't fail this one!
